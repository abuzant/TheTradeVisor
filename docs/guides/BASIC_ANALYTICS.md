# Basic Analytics Guide

## Overview
This guide introduces the basic analytics features of TheTradeVisor platform.

## Getting Started with Analytics

### Accessing Analytics
1. Click "Analytics" in the top navigation
2. Select time period (default: Last 30 days)
3. Choose accounts to analyze
4. Click "Apply Filters"

### Key Metrics Overview

#### Performance Metrics
- **Total Return**: Overall profit/loss percentage
- **Win Rate**: Percentage of profitable trades
- **Profit Factor**: Total profit / Total loss
- **Sharpe Ratio**: Risk-adjusted returns

#### Risk Metrics
- **Max Drawdown**: Largest peak-to-trough decline
- **Average Drawdown**: Typical decline from peaks
- **Recovery Factor**: Return / Max Drawdown
- **Risk of Ruin**: Probability of account depletion

## Trade Analysis

### Trade Distribution
- **Winning Trades**: Number and percentage
- **Losing Trades**: Number and percentage
- **Breakeven Trades**: Neither profit nor loss
- **Average Trade**: Typical P&L per trade

### Profit/Loss Analysis
- **Gross Profit**: Total from winning trades
- **Gross Loss**: Total from losing trades
- **Net Profit**: Overall result
- **Average Win**: Typical winning trade
- **Average Loss**: Typical losing trade

## Symbol Performance

### Top Performing Symbols
View your best and worst performing symbols:
- **By Profit**: Most profitable instruments
- **By Win Rate**: Highest success percentage
- **By Volume**: Most traded instruments
- **By Frequency**: Most actively traded

### Symbol Details
For each symbol, you can see:
- Total trades
- Win rate
- Total profit/loss
- Average profit/loss
- Best and worst trades

## Time Analysis

### Trading Sessions
Analyze performance by trading session:
- **Asian Session**: Sydney, Tokyo
- **European Session**: London, Frankfurt
- **American Session**: New York, Chicago
- **Overlapping Sessions**: High liquidity periods

### Day of Week
Discover patterns by day:
- **Monday**: Week opening trends
- **Tuesday-Friday**: Regular trading days
- **Weekend**: Closed markets (some crypto)

### Hourly Analysis
Find your best trading hours:
- **Market Open**: High volatility
- **Market Close**: Position squaring
- **News Releases**: Economic impact
- **Quiet Hours**: Low activity

## Position Analysis

### Long vs Short Positions
Compare performance of:
- **Buy positions**: Profit from rising prices
- **Sell positions**: Profit from falling prices
- **Hedged positions**: Risk management

### Position Duration
Analyze hold times:
- **Scalping**: Seconds to minutes
- **Day Trading**: Intraday positions
- **Swing Trading**: Days to weeks
- **Position Trading**: Weeks to months

## Risk Management Analytics

### Position Sizing
- **Average Position Size**: Typical lot size
- **Position Size Distribution**: Size ranges
- **Risk per Trade**: Percentage at risk
- **Correlation Analysis**: Diversification metrics

### Stop Loss Analysis
- **Stop Loss Hit Rate**: How often stops trigger
- **Average Stop Distance**: Typical stop placement
- **Stop Loss Efficiency**: Effectiveness of stops
- **Trailing Stops**: Dynamic stop usage

## Filtering and Segmentation

### Filter Options

### Segmentation
Analyze subsets of your trades:
- **By Symbol**: Individual instrument analysis
- **By Session**: Time-based performance
- **By Size**: Position size impact
- **By Duration**: Hold time effects

## Best Practices

### Data Quality
- Ensure EA is running continuously
- Verify all accounts are connected
- Check for missing data periods
- Validate trade accuracy

### Analysis Tips
- Use multiple timeframes
- Compare relative performance
- Look for patterns, not anomalies
- Consider market conditions

### Common Pitfalls
- Overfitting to past data
- Ignoring risk metrics
- Sample size too small
- Confirmation bias

## Troubleshooting

### Data Issues
- **Missing Trades**: Check EA connection
- **Incorrect Values**: Verify symbol mapping
- **Time Zone**: Check local settings
- **Currency**: Verify display currency

### Performance Issues
- **Slow Loading**: Reduce date range
- **Charts Not Loading**: Clear browser cache
- **Export Errors**: Check file permissions
- **Calculation Errors**: Refresh data
